# Meridian Global Opportunities Fund

## Q4 2025 Performance Summary

The Fund returned 8.4% net of fees in the fourth quarter, outperforming its benchmark by 210 basis points. Performance was led by an overweight position in industrials and a well-timed rotation out of long-duration government bonds ahead of the December rate decision.

Net assets under management closed the quarter at $2.13 billion, up from $1.94 billion at the end of the third quarter. Net inflows of $124 million were concentrated in the institutional share class, while the retail class saw modest redemptions.

- Top contributor: Ashford Industrial Group, which added 38 basis points

- Top detractor: Kestrel Biosciences, which cost 22 basis points

- Portfolio turnover for the quarter was 14%
